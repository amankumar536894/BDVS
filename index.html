About Us

Bihar Dalit Vikas Samiti

Bihar Dalit Vikas Samiti (BDVS) is a grassroots NGO committed to uplifting marginalized communities across Bihar. Since 1982, we've been working relentlessly to promote social justice, access to quality education, healthcare, and livelihood opportunities.

Our mission is to ensure equality, dignity, and a better future for every individual who has been historically oppressed or ignored.
