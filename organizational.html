Bihar Dalit Vikas Samiti is a grassroots NGO ommitted to uplifting marginalized communities, especially Dalits, across Bihar. Since our inception, we've been working relentlessly to promote social justice, access to quality education, healthcare, and vocational training. Our goal is to ensure equality, dignity, and a better future for every individual who has been historically oppressed or ignored.
