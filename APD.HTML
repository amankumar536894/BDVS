APD (Ambedkar Prerna Dal)

For Quality Education and Competency Enhancement

Ambedkar Prerna Dal (APD) is BDVS's flagship education program dedicated to providing quality education and skill development opportunities to Dalit and underprivileged children in Bihar. Inspired by Dr. B.R. Ambedkar's vision of education as the key to social empowerment, we work to bridge the educational gap and create future leaders from marginalized communities.

Quality Education

Comprehensive academic support and coaching for school subjects

Digital Literacy

Computer education and digital skills for the 21st century

Leadership Development

Building confidence and leadership skills among young students

Program Impact

Through APD, we've successfully supported over 500 students in their educational journey, providing them with not just academic knowledge but also the confidence to dream big and the skills to achieve their aspirations. Our focus is on holistic development that combines education with social awareness and leadership qualities.
